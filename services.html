Jyotish Praveena

This course is offered in three languages, (i.e., in English, Hindi and Telugu).

Examinations for this course is conducted twice a year in June and December at Bharatiya Vidya Bhawan, Hyderguda, Hyderabad.

Subjects

    First Semester

    • Paper-I - General Astrology, Astronomy Relevant to Astrology
    • Paper-II - Mathematical Astrology, Predictive Astrology

    Second Semester

    • Paper-III - Deliniation and Dynamic Configuration, Dasa and Basic Transit
    • Paper-IV - Tajik System, Muhurta

  • Download Syllabus and Reference Books

Jyotish Visharada

This course is offered in two languages, (i.e., in English and Hindi).

Examinations for this course is conducted twice a year in June and December at Bharatiya Vidya Bhawan, Hyderguda, Hyderabad.

Subjects

    First Semester

    • Paper-I - Ashtakavarga, Horary
    • Paper-II - Shadbala, Judgement of Bhavas
    • Paper-III - Longevity, Medical Astrology

    Second Semester

    • Paper-IV - Dasa System, Predicting & Timing Events and Transits
    • Paper-V - Tajik System, Muhurta
    • Paper-VI - Composite Techniques of Prediction, Mundane Astrology

  • Download Syllabus and Reference Books
